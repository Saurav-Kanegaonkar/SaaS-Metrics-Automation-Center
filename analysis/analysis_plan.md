# Analysis Plan

## Business question

Which accounts, metrics, and recurring stakeholder reports should a SaaS integration analytics team prioritize so leaders can trust self serve reporting and act faster?

## Analytical framework

1. Build the account grain by joining account attributes to daily integration flow, usage, support, data quality, freshness, risk, and expansion signals.
2. Score account priority from operational risk, revenue opportunity, and metric trust gap.
3. Audit metric definitions for owner coverage, freshness SLA misses, certification state, and quality thresholds.
4. Rank recurring reports by manual toil, dashboard reuse, data readiness, executive visibility, and AI assist fit.
5. Convert the top queues into stakeholder ready actions that separate metric fixes, reliability escalations, and expansion analysis.

## Interview defense

The artifact is intentionally not just a dashboard. It shows the full insight delivery lifecycle a data analyst would own: translate ambiguous questions, define metrics, validate data quality, build self serve views, identify anomalies, and automate repeated reporting workflows.
